Zocats

Ninja Cats vs Zombie Dogs and their foul allies

Story

The mystical seven kittens of the elemental cat god have been catnapped by the dark forces of the canine necromancer Sseth.

If you do not save them he will use their powers to bring back the extinct "Monkeykind", the world enders, who were once his masters and "best friend".

Our best operatives claim that Sseth works in collusion with the "Ruinous Rats"; the rats living in the forbidden "Monkeykind" ruins from before the great Apecalypse

Controls

Move by using the ARROW keys or ASWD

Goals

  • Collect the seven kittens by navigating to their position
  • Avoid the zombie chasing you by not navigating to its position
  • You have nine lives
  • You have a sixth sense
  • You have no internal vision [ie. 'minimap']
  • Your efforts will be in vain
